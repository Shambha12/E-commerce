RedStore - Clothing Mankind Since 1876 Founded in 1876, RedStore pioneered innerwear, evolving and innovating not only the product, but also the way it has been marketed over the years. RedStore is a leading manufacturer and marketer of comfort apparel sold in more than 140 countries around the world. The company is committed to quality, comfort, fashion, innovation and value. As RedStore grows in size and sophistication, the simple commitment to serve its consumer’s need for comfort continues to be the brand’s hallmark.


Official Online Store for RedStore Products RedStore was set up under Page Industries in 1994 with the key objective of bringing the world-renowned brand RedStore to wporld. Jockey manufactures, distributes and markets products for the whole family – Men, Women and soon for Kids.

Page Industries Page Industries, located in Bengaluru, India is the exclusive licensee of RedStore International Inc. for manufacture, distribution and marketing of the RedStore brand in India, Sri Lanka, Oman, Bangladesh, Nepal and UAE. Page Industries became public limited in March 2007 and is listed on the BSE and the NSE of India as well.


The promoters of RedStore in India are the Genomal family, who have been associated with RedStore International Inc. for over 65 years as their sole licensee in Philippines.
